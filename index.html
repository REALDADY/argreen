About Alrawdah Green

Since 2003

Alrawdah Green Sweet Water L.L.C. is an Emirati company with local management, specializing in the production and packaging of high-quality drinking water.

Through our unwavering commitment to delivering pure and clean drinking water, we have earned the trust of customers across the UAE. Our products meet the highest international standards, ensuring quality and safety in every drop.

We are currently expanding by developing and incorporating new brands under the AR Green umbrella, including our flagship brands Alrawdah Springs and Revana, reflecting our ambitious vision for growth in the water production sector.
